What's Inside

Everything in one place

From carry laws to CCW checklists, 2A Base brings together source-linked reference material organized the way you actually need it.

🛡️
State Law Library
Browse firearm laws by state across six categories: Eligibility, Carry Laws, Storage & Safety, Prohibited Weapons, Ammunition, and Rights.
📋
CCW Roadmap
Step-by-step guides for obtaining a concealed carry permit. Track your progress with a built-in checklist linked to official application sources.
🗺️
County Guidance
County-level law data and issuing authority information for your home county. Know who processes your application and what local rules apply.
🔔
Law Update Alerts
Opt in to push notifications when firearm laws change in your state. Monitored by automated scanning and reviewed before publishing.
🔁
Reciprocity Reference
Quick-reference reciprocity data showing which states honor your home state permit, with direct links to verify against official sources.
🔖
Bookmarks & Watchlist
Save laws you reference often and watch multiple states at once. Unread indicators surface new or recently changed laws at a glance.
