## Description
2A Base is a firearm regulation reference app for users who want organized, source-linked firearm regulation information without searching through scattered agency pages.

WHAT'S INSIDE
- State firearm law summaries for supported jurisdictions
- Federal reference topics covering NFA items, prohibited persons, interstate transport, and related federal rules
- County-level CCW process notes where local data is available
- Reciprocity reference for supported permit states
- Eligibility and transfer compliance checklist
- CCW application checklist and optional permit-renewal reminders
- Source-linked legal update feed
- Optional alerts for enacted laws, pending bills, and court rulings

SUPPORTED AT LAUNCH
2A Base currently focuses on federal law plus these states: AZ, CA, CO, CT, FL, GA, HI, IL, NC, NJ, NY, OH, TN, TX, VA, and WA.

STAY CURRENT
Firearm laws change frequently. 2A Base monitors selected official government and court sources, detects source changes, and routes potential updates into a review queue before publication.

PRO MEMBERSHIP
2A Base Pro unlocks enhanced county data, source-linked update history, daily update access, optional push alerts, and expanded coverage as new jurisdictions are added.

IMPORTANT
2A Base provides general firearm law information for educational and reference purposes only. It is not legal advice. Laws change frequently and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or different at the city or county level. Always verify current law with official government sources and consult a licensed firearms attorney before making carry, transport, storage, transfer, or compliance decisions. You are solely responsible for complying with all applicable laws.

## App Review Notes Draft
2A Base is a reference app for firearm law summaries, CCW checklists, county process notes, reciprocity references, and source-linked legal update alerts. It does not sell firearms, ammunition, accessories, training, or physical goods. It does not facilitate firearm transactions, dealer listings, product listings, price comparisons, or marketplace activity. It does not provide legal advice.

No account is required. Push notifications are optional and are only requested after the user enables notification preferences. The app includes accessible Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and legal disclaimer links in the Account tab.

Pro subscription access is handled through Apple in-app purchase via RevenueCat. The Restore Purchase button is available on the subscription screen and in Account.

For review, the app can be used without login credentials. If subscriptions are not yet approved, reviewers can inspect free content and the paywall behavior without purchasing.
